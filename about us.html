COMPANY PROFILE

Easylicious began in December 2020 when we decided to hop on current trend in which people prefer staying at home than going outside. We tried to grasp the situation to gain the benefit from it by starting our own business.With that, we developed an idea to establish a frozen food company as partnership business and named it Easylicious.

we specialized in manufacturing of frozen food such as curry puff, samosa, and dumpling. Easyliscious Frozen Food located at No 3G, 15, Jalan Dagangan 2, Pusat Bandar Bertam Perdana, 13200, Kepala Batas, Pulau Pinang.
